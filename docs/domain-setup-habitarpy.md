# Domain setup - habitarpy.com

Habitar now has its own domain:

```text
habitarpy.com
```

This keeps Habitar separate from ASULUGAR while allowing the app name to remain
`Habitar` in Google Play and App Store.

## Recommended public URLs

- Website: `https://habitarpy.com`
- Privacy policy: `https://habitarpy.com/privacy`
- Terms: `https://habitarpy.com/terms`
- Support email: `soporte@habitarpy.com`

## DNS plan

Keep Namecheap BasicDNS for now.

Next setup options:

1. Use a simple static site host for privacy/terms and landing page.
2. Point Namecheap DNS records to that host.
3. Configure email forwarding or mailbox for `soporte@habitarpy.com`.

## Minimum site pages needed for Google Play

- `/privacy`
- `/terms`
- Home page with app name, support contact and store status.

The legal drafts are in:

- `docs/privacy-policy-draft.md`
- `docs/terms-of-use-draft.md`
